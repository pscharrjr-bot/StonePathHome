About StonePathHome

StonePathHome is being built as a practical home-buying tool brand that starts with mortgage payment estimates and grows into more useful planning resources over time.

Why this site exists

Buying a home is one of the biggest financial decisions most people will make, but many calculators either oversimplify the monthly cost or bury users under clutter. StonePathHome aims to make the decision process easier by starting with clean, understandable mortgage estimates.

What StonePathHome focuses on

The first version focuses on the main mortgage calculator experience: home price, down payment, interest rate, loan term, taxes, insurance, HOA, and PMI in one place.

Where it can grow

Future versions may add affordability tools, refinance calculators, rent-vs-buy comparisons, and more educational content for buyers who want better context around their monthly payment decisions.

Core principle

Keep the site useful, clear, and trustworthy. Good finance tools do not need to feel intimidating. They need to help people understand the numbers well enough to ask better questions and make smarter decisions.
