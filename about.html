About the site

Helping you understand the true cost of homeownership

StonePathHome is being developed as a practical home-buying resource that combines calculators, educational guides, and clearer affordability framing in one place.

About StonePathHome

StonePathHome exists to help buyers understand monthly payment reality more clearly before making one of the biggest financial decisions of their lives.

Why this site exists

Buying a home is one of the biggest financial decisions most people will ever make, but many mortgage tools still reduce that decision to an overly simple monthly number. StonePathHome exists to make the process calmer, clearer, and more realistic.

The goal is not just to calculate a payment. The goal is to help people understand what the payment really represents once taxes, insurance, HOA dues, and PMI enter the picture.

What StonePathHome focuses on

The site focuses on mortgage planning tools and buyer education, including calculators and a growing set of guides around PMI, escrow, insurance, taxes, closing costs, and affordability tradeoffs.

How the site is built

StonePathHome is being developed as a clean informational publishing site with calculators and educational resources working together. The long-term goal is a stronger resource library that helps buyers navigate financing and ownership costs more realistically.

Core principle

Keep the site useful, clear, and trustworthy. Good home-buying tools should help people understand the numbers well enough to make smarter decisions and recognize what still needs to be confirmed with professionals.
