Privacy Policy

StonePathHome is designed as a simple educational mortgage planning resource and this page outlines how basic information may be handled.

General use

StonePathHome is designed to work as a public educational tool. You do not need to create an account to use the mortgage calculator.

Most calculator activity is intended to happen directly in your browser using the values you enter on the page.

Technical data

Like most websites, hosting providers, analytics tools, or related services may collect limited technical information such as browser type, device type, approximate location derived from IP address, and general usage patterns.

Emails and messages

If you contact the site by email, the information you provide may be used to respond, review a problem, or improve the site.

Future features

If advertising, affiliate offers, analytics, email capture, or other features are added later, this policy may be updated to reflect those changes.

Policy updates

This privacy policy may be revised as the site changes over time.
