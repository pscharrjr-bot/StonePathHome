# StonePathHome

StonePathHome v1 is a lightweight static mortgage calculator site built from the unit-conversion-toolkit template.

## v1 pages
- Home
- Mortgage Calculator
- About
- Contact
- Privacy Policy
- Terms of Use

## calculator inputs
- Home price
- Down payment
- Interest rate
- Loan term
- Annual property taxes
- Annual homeowners insurance
- Monthly HOA
- Monthly PMI

## calculator outputs
- Estimated monthly payment
- Principal and interest
- Taxes
- Insurance
- HOA
- PMI
- Loan amount
- Total interest
- Total paid
- First-month amortization snapshot
