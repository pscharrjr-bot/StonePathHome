What StonePathHome helps you estimate

A mortgage payment is not just principal and interest. This experience is built to reflect the costs buyers most often underestimate while still keeping the interface calm and readable.

Principal and interest

Estimate the core monthly payment using home price, down payment, interest rate, and loan term.

Taxes and insurance

Factor in annual property taxes and homeowners insurance so the monthly estimate feels more like the real commitment.

HOA and PMI

Include optional monthly HOA dues and private mortgage insurance to avoid false affordability signals.

A more premium planning experience

StonePathHome is shifting from a basic utility look toward a calmer editorial feel: softer contrast, more intentional typography, and a layout that suggests trust instead of clutter. Finance tools should feel reassuring, not stressful.

Who this is for

  • First-time home buyers comparing price ranges
  • People trying to understand how taxes and insurance affect affordability
  • Buyers choosing between different down payment paths
  • Anyone who wants a more realistic payment estimate before talking to lenders
