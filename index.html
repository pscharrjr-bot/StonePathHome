What StonePathHome helps you estimate

A mortgage payment is not just principal and interest. StonePathHome is built to reflect the costs buyers most often underestimate while keeping the experience calm, readable, and practical.

Principal and interest

Estimate the core monthly payment using home price, down payment, interest rate, and loan term.

Taxes and insurance

Include property taxes and homeowners insurance to get closer to the real cost of ownership.

HOA and PMI

Factor in optional HOA dues and private mortgage insurance so the payment estimate is more realistic.

What these tools help you do

  • Estimate a fuller monthly payment from a target home price
  • Work backward from a comfortable monthly budget
  • Compare affordability scenarios more realistically
  • Understand how taxes, insurance, HOA, and PMI shape the payment

Who this is for

  • First-time home buyers comparing price ranges
  • People trying to understand how taxes and insurance affect affordability
  • Buyers choosing between different down payment paths
  • Anyone who wants a more realistic payment estimate before talking to lenders
