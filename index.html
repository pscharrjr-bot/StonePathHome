Simple mortgage payment estimates for real home-buying decisions

StonePathHome is built to help you estimate monthly mortgage costs without clutter. Use the calculator to combine principal, interest, property taxes, homeowners insurance, HOA fees, and PMI into one clear monthly payment view.

What StonePathHome helps you estimate

A home payment is more than just the loan itself. This v1 launch focuses on the costs most buyers actually need to see together before making a decision.

Principal and interest

Estimate the core monthly payment on your mortgage loan using home price, down payment, rate, and term.

Taxes and insurance

Add annual property taxes and homeowners insurance to get closer to a realistic monthly payment.

HOA and PMI

Include optional monthly HOA dues and private mortgage insurance to avoid underestimating the payment.

Built for a clean first version

This site starts with one strong tool instead of trying to be everything at once. The goal is to give buyers a fast, understandable estimate they can actually use while comparing homes, budgets, or financing scenarios.

Who this is for

  • First-time home buyers comparing price ranges
  • People trying to understand how taxes and insurance change affordability
  • Buyers deciding between different down payment options
  • Anyone who wants a quick monthly payment estimate before going deeper
