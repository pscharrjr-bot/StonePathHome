How property taxes affect mortgage payments

Property taxes can move the monthly payment far more than many buyers expect.

How property taxes affect mortgage payments

Property taxes are commonly collected as part of the monthly housing payment through escrow. That means they can raise the real monthly payment even on a fixed-rate loan.

Why they matter

Two homes at the same price can feel very different in monthly cost if one sits in a higher-tax area.

Planning takeaway

Never set a budget using principal and interest alone. Taxes should be included from the beginning or the estimate will be misleading.
